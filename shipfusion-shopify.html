Goal

Businesses use Shopify to quickly and easily create an ecommerce website. Users are able to download apps based on their needs. project overview

As a result of the deterioration of Hong Kong's democracy and its citizens' peaceful protest that lasted several months, Canada has introduced a special immigration program to Hong Kong citizens seeking asylum in Canada. The vision of CANHK is to proactively create a digital platform for online learning and knowledge sharing. The platform is supported by two pillars: 1) training material is curated for Our Common Calling associated churches from across Canada in preparation to receive refugees, 2) essential information is curated for asylum seekers to accelerate their acclimation to Canadian society. CANHK is a pilot project and after project launch, it strives to be a platform that proactively supports all future refugees seeking asylum in Canada.

Problem Statememt

Asylum seekers are especially vulnerable because they face many barriers as they enter a foreign country with its own culture, language and societal norms, and having to leave their home country and everything they know behind. Refugees have limited access, knowledge, and social support to settle in a foreign country. It's extremely time-consuming and frustrating to find the essential information needed. CANHK has an abundance of information to share with refugees coming to Canada. However, the information architecture and user interface lacked organization and clear call-to-action, minimizing potential usefulness.

How might we present the information clearly and in an organized manner in order to help people easily access the information they need when they need it?

Solution

Create a clean and easy-to-navigate user interface that allows CANHK that displays various types of information in an cohesive manner. In addition, guide the user through the journey of settling in Canada, so they feel supported and welcomed. Motivate the user to revisit CANHK's website they get adjusted to their life in Canada and providing a sense of community to the newcomers.

Design Process

double diamond design process

I used the Double Diamond design process to guide me through product design cycle. The process starts by expanding the understanding of the users needs through research and acquiring research data. Next, the broad data I found through the user research is synthesized, refined, and made meaningful. Through empathy, the problem statement is clearly defined. Ideating solutions that solves the problem in a creative way that resonates with the end-users. Finally deliver a product that is tested and validated with end-users which meets their needs.
